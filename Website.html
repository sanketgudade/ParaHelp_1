Welcome to ParaHelp!

At ParaHelp, our mission is to provide persons with mobility impairments the tools and support they need to communicate effectively and manage their daily activities with enhanced independence.

We leverage two innovative technologies to empower our users:

1.Capacitive Touch Sensor – This device allows users to control and communicate using finger touch only.It offers a user-friendly interface for various tasks.

Capacitive Touch Sensor



Eye-Blinking Input Glasses –A specialized tool designed to help users communicate through eye blinks, enabling seamless interaction with their environment.

Eye-Blinking Input Glasses

Our Vision

We envision a world where persons with mobility impairments can lead fulfilling lives with dignity and independence. Our goal is to break down barriers and create inclusive solutions.
