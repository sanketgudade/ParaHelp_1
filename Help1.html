Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I set up my capacitive touch sensor?

    You have to check whether the capacitive touch sensor is located near the patient, ensuring they can use it without any difficulties.

  • How does eye-blinking selection will be work?

    Our system uses advanced algorithms to detect and interpret eye blinks. Simply calibrate the system to your specific blinking patterns, and then you can select options by blinking.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • My capacitive touch sensor is not responding.

    Ensure the device is properly connected to the ParaHelp system. Check for any physical obstructions or damage.

  • The eye-blink selection system is not registering blinks correctly.

    Check whether the patient wears the glasses properly, ensuring the sensor can detect their eye motions.
